Rock Paper Scissors

4 Player Multiplayer

📜 How to Play

  • Each player chooses Rock ✊, Paper ✋, or Scissors ✌️ in their own box.
  • Click Submit after choosing.
  • When all 4 players have submitted, the results will be shown.
  • Winner is decided by classic rules:
    • Rock beats Scissors
    • Scissors beats Paper
    • Paper beats Rock
  • If multiple players win, it’s a tie between them.
