How to Play

Click on each player box to activate it, then select Rock, Paper, or Scissors for that player

After all players have made their selections, the winner will be announced automatically

Rock beats Scissors, Scissors beats Paper, Paper beats Rock

If multiple players choose the same winning hand, they all win the round
